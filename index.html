FAQ

Orders

How do I place an order?
Browse products, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery details, and complete payment. It's that simple.
Can I track my order?
Yes. You can track your order through your account dashboard. You'll also receive order updates via email and WhatsApp.
Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Once dispatched, cancellation is not possible. Please contact us as soon as possible if you wish to cancel.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, Debit Cards, Credit Cards, and Cash on Delivery (where available).
Is online payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways. Your financial details are never stored on our servers.

Products

Are the product photos real?
We strive to display products as accurately as possible. Minor color variations may occur due to different screen settings and lighting.
Will sold-out products come back?
Some products may be restocked. Use the "Notify Me" option on the product page when available, and we'll let you know when it's back.
